Every blog starts with a hello world post, so here's mine.

## Why I'm writing

I've been building software for a while now, and I've noticed that writing helps me think more clearly. This blog is my attempt to externalize that thinking — to capture ideas while they're fresh and share them with whoever finds them useful.

## What I'll write about

Mostly things I'm working on or learning:

- **AI agents** — building systems that reason and act autonomously
- **Backend engineering** — APIs, databases, distributed systems
- **Developer tooling** — things that make the development experience better
- **Occasional opinion pieces** — on software craft and industry trends

## How often

No promises. Quality over cadence.
